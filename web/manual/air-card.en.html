For better AIR in your room

AIR CARD feature lets your devices work together follow Daikin’s logic. One adds aroma while the other cools the room or boosts airflow. Creating the perfect atmosphere for any activity.

AIR CARD is available in Online mode only

AIR CARD

To start using AIRCARD make sure you are in online mode and finish the initial set up or see how to set up Now you can follow step down below

1. On Air management page, select your preferred place on the top.
2. Tap to your room.
3.Now you’re inside the room. The AIR CARD is on top of the screen. You can choose any AIR CARD as you like.

4. To use AIR CARD, set duration time and setting device operation such as adjust temperature, airflow rate or just use the setting as it is. You can select how long you want this specific AIR CARD to run or let it operate continuously as long as maximum capacity of devices.

Note : in Sleep AIR CARD you need to set wake up time.

5. Next set device operation such as adjust temperature, airflow rate by tapping at the device or just use the setting as it is. If you have one more types of device, you can choose just one device to run by tapping “Disabled” the device you don’t want it to run.
6. When you’re ready , tap “Start this AIR CARD”. The screen will start counting your set time.
7. To stop, tap “Stop this AIR CARD”
