Features

ENERGY MONITORING

Set the days of the week and times that specific operations start and end, and other detailed settings.

Overviews

Details of on-demand billing, electricity cost, and electrical restrictions — with the flexibility to view data by place, room, or device. You can also explore visual insights through charts that show both current usage and historical trends.

Access Modes

AP mode

Connect directly to the Wi-Fi Adapter without Internet, but Energy demand summary cannot be generated. You can only view the kWh data for the current month and the previous month.

Offline mode

Connect the Wi-Fi Adapter to the Internet to view more detailed usage activity, grouped by device. You can view kWh data in both month and year views, and also review historical usage.

Online mode

You can view usage data and set monthly usage targets, or choose your preferred currency. The data is calculated and displayed as a pie chart for each device, making it detailed and easy to understand. You can also view data in both month and year views, as well as review historical usage.

3 Levels of View

Place view

View the combined usage of all devices within the place.

Room view

View the combined usage of all devices within the room.

Device view

Allows users to see detailed usage, status, and settings for a specific device.

Energy Usage settings

To make the Monthly Target effective, enable Bills On Demand.

1. Tab “ ” Settings for the energy device is at the top-right corner.
2. Follow the steps below.

1. Energy usage limitation

Select the Bills On Demand option. Then, configure the Target settings and enable Bills On Demand to activate the feature.

. Warning Only : Notifications will be sent when consumption reaches 50%, 70%, 90%, and 100% of the daily target.
. Automatic Control : Adjust the energy consumption automatically when it reaches 50%, 70%, 90%, and 100% of the daily target.
. Automatic Control with force off : Unit will be Turned Off when the consumption exceeds 100% of the daily target.

Note 1 : Automatic Control / Automatic Control with force off may cause users to feel uncomfortably warm during operation.
Note 2 : When Automatic Control is selected, the consumption may exceed the target depended on your usage.

2.Cost setting

Set the desired value for each month to control usage.

1. Tap the  Edit Cost  button to start the setup
2. Set the monthly target based on your preferred usage
3. Set the unit price — by default, it is pre-filled with  27.00
4.Tab save

. Monthly target : can only be set if the value is higher than the Current Usage.
. Electricity Unit Price : refers to the electricity price per unit.

Note : The cost setting can be adjusted only when the energy usage limitation is disabled.

5. After completing the setup, enable the toggle switch to start using it.

You will be able to edit the cost only when the Bills On Demand is disabled.

1. Disable energy usage limitation
2. After disabling the energy usage limitation, you can edit all related settings.
