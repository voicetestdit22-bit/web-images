Add Schedule Flow

1. The Add schedule tab on your Scheduling page.
2. Follow the steps below.

• Where : First, select Where to choose the room where you want to add the schedule.
• What : Choose whether to set a Timer for the device or a Timer for the AIR CARD.
Note : The operation settings may vary depending on the device.
Note : CARD functionality depends on whether the device supports it.
• When : Choose which days of the week and what time you want the device to work.

3. After finishing the setup, tap “Save this schedule.”
4. You will get the scheduled task on the Scheduling page.

Edit Schedule

1. Tab schedule task on the scheduling page.
2. Edit or delete your schedule.
